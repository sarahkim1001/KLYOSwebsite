Welcome to Klyos Capital Investment

Located in Palo Alto, CA—the heart of Silicon Valley—Klyos Capital is a growth fund focused on investing in the transformative sector of Artificial Intelligence.

Our investment philosophy is driven by clarity and conviction. We leverage the long-term transformative potential of AI by identifying undervalued companies and often overlooked catalysts. Through evidence-based financial research and a keen awareness of market distortions, we aim to capture opportunities with substantial upside before they are widely recognized.

At Klyos Capital, we don't just invest in businesses; we invest in their potential to reshape industries and create enduring value.
