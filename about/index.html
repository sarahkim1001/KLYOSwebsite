Meet the Team

Based in Silicon Valley, California, Klyos Capital Investment is more than a private equity firm—we are partners in progress, united by a shared vision of enduring impact. Founded by Julius and Alon, our boutique firm is built on the strength of brotherhood and a relentless commitment to transformative growth. Drawing from the timeless Greek ethos of legacy, we forge deep, trust-based partnerships with visionary companies worldwide, guiding them toward sustainable success.

Our investment philosophy is rooted in clarity and conviction. We harness the long-term transformative power of artificial intelligence, focusing on companies the market undervalues and catalysts often overlooked. Guided by evidence-based research and a keen understanding of market distortions caused by “noise,” we aim to capture asymmetric upside before opportunities are widely recognized. At Klyos, we don’t just invest in businesses—we invest in their potential to redefine industries and create lasting value.

As young investors, Julius and Alon bring a dynamic blend of bold vision and disciplined execution to Klyos. Julius, our Managing Partner, drives strategic direction with an intuitive grasp of emerging trends and a passion for forging meaningful partnerships. His leadership transforms bold ideas into actionable strategies, positioning Klyos at the forefront of AI-driven innovation. Alon, our Investment Director, complements this vision with razor-sharp analytical prowess, uncovering hidden opportunities in undervalued markets. His rigorous, data-driven approach ensures every investment is grounded in insight, maximizing impact and returns. Together, their synergy as a team redefines private equity, blending youthful audacity with timeless wisdom to create a legacy of transformative growth.
