Meet the Team

Julius Kim and Alon Ben-Shachar are the co-founders of Klyos Capital, a dynamic investment firm at the forefront of emerging technology sectors. Both passionate about innovation and driven by a shared vision, Julius and Alon bring complementary skills and insights to their leadership roles at Klyos.

Growing up with an insatiable curiosity for new trends, Julius was always drawn to the cutting-edge developments in technology and blockchain. His keen eye for identifying hidden opportunities led him to found Klyos at the age of 24, with a focus on pioneering investments in artificial intelligence. Julius's background in capital markets equips him with a strategic mindset, allowing him to approach investment opportunities with a unique and insightful perspective.

Similarly, Alon developed a deep appreciation for evidence-based decision-making through his observations of prospect theory and distorted valuations in his family's investments. At 23, he co-founded Klyos alongside Julius, driven by a commitment to challenge assumptions and build conviction on hard evidence. Alon's vast network of savvy tech investors and his analytical acumen provide a solid foundation for the firm's investment strategies.

Together, Julius and Alon create a symbiotic leadership dynamic that combines Julius's innovative foresight with Alon's evidence-driven approach. As they navigate the ever-evolving landscape of AI and other emerging technologies, their shared mission is to uncover and nurture the most promising opportunities, ensuring that Klyos remains at the vanguard of transformative investment opportunities.
